Collaborate Around the World or in the Same Room

jott.ly allows you to write with anyone, anywhere.

write

jott.ly is a simple and minimal web application that gets out of the way to allow you to write down your ideas and let them grow.  learn more

collaborate

jott.ly is built around the idea that documents are meant to be worked on together, regardless of whether the other person is in the next room or a different continent. learn more

share

Once started, documents can be shared with yourself, your group, or your larger social network to encourage cooperation and collaboration. learn more

Using jott.ly is simple and easy

  • Documents are publishable on jott.ly in a variety of formats and layouts

  • Edit and change any document you’ve been invited in to, including your own

  • Social connections within the service allow you to see, view and help with what your friends are working on

  • jott.ly’s layout is focused around typography and the beauty of writing
